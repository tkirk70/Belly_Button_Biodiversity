Scinetists are studying ways of creating protein-rich food from sources other than livestock in an effort to decrease the human footprint on the environment. Roza is a microbiologist studying common bacteria that live on the human body. She is specifically looking for microorganisms which synthesize proteins that taste like beef. Her random study is identifying all the different bacterial species which live in the human belly-button. Samples were taken from 153 participants and revelaed at least 80 different species of bacteria. Maybe someday they will be used to grow your hamburger.
